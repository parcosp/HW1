What is the Stock Market?

Stock markets are where individual and instutional investors
come together to buy and sell shares in a public venue which
is done now all electronically.
Share prices are set by supply and demand in the market as
buyers and sellers place ordersd.

Type of investors

There are a few different types of investors when it comes
to the stock market.

  • Long Term Traders: Where an investor will hold their posistion
    for years or more.
  • Swinger Traders: Where an investor will hold their position for
    days to weeks to months.
  • Day Traders: Where an investor will buy and sell their position
    in the same day.
